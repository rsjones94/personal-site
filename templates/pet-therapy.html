Pet Therapy

Once a week I take Fred to the hospital to visit with patients and staff. Fred is a registered therapy dog with Intermountain Therapy Animals (ITA) and a 100% certified ham. Though he's a pembroke corgi, Fred has a cartilage deficiency in his ears so they don't stand up. Some people think that makes him even cuter!
