fuzzyPEACH: Fuzzy Logic Simulation of Deltaic Deposition

As an undergraduate, I took over development of a MATLAB tool called fuzzyPEACH (Fuzzy Predictive Earth Analysis Constrained by Heuristics). Originally written by Dr. Jeffrey Warren as a way to simulate stratigraphic architecture developed in deltaic depositional systems on passive margins (e.g., the East China Sea), my job was to update the model to better represent more diverse systems.
